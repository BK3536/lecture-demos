Shannon-Hartley Theorem
C = B log2(1 + SNR)

Shannon Limit:
Min Eb/N0 for reliable comms:
Eb/N0 ≥ (2C/B - 1) / (C/B)

At C/B → 0:
Eb/N0 ≥ ln(2) = -1.59 dB

Key insight: You can always trade bandwidth for SNR and vice versa — Shannon doesn't care about the split.
